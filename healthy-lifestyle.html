Healthy Lifestyle

Nutrition

Eating a balanced diet fuels the body and supports overall health. Focus on a variety of whole foods, including fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, whole grains, and healthy fats. Limit processed foods, added sugars, and excessive sodium. Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water. Portion control and mindful eating are key to maintaining a healthy weight and ensuring proper nutrient intake.

Exercise

Regular physical activity boosts cardiovascular health, strengthens muscles, enhances flexibility, and supports mental well-being. Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate aerobic exercise or 75 minutes of vigorous activity weekly, combined with strength training twice a week. Activities like walking, running, swimming, yoga, and weightlifting can improve energy levels and reduce the risk of chronic diseases.
